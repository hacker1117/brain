# ideas/ — Raw Ideas

## What goes here
Undeveloped ideas, sparks, hunches. Not yet synthesized into concepts.

## What does NOT go here
- Fully formed concepts (→ concepts/)
- Projects (→ projects/)

## Rule
Ideas either grow into concepts/projects or get discarded. Don't let this become a graveyard.
